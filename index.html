About Us

Welocme To Valluru's Restaturant


Andhra cuisine, from Andhra Pradesh, India, is known for its bold flavors and spiciness, with key ingredients like rice, chilies, tamarind, curry leaves, and mustard seeds. Popular dishes include Pulihora (tamarind rice), Pesarattu (green gram dosa), Gongura Pachadi (sorrel leaves chutney), Kodi Kura (spicy chicken curry), Andhra Chepala Pulusu (fish curry).


Andhra snacks and sweets are equally renowned, featuring items like Mirchi Bajji (chili fritters), Punugulu (deep-fried fritters), and Ariselu (rice flour and jaggery sweet). Accompaniments like Avakaya (mango pickle) and Appadam (crispy lentil papad) are staples, enhancing the meal experience with their distinct flavors.


The cuisine varies by region, with Rayalaseema known for fiery dishes, Coastal Andhra for seafood. Meals are traditionally served on banana leaves during special occasions and are often elaborate, ending with yogurt or buttermilk to cool the palate after the spicy fare.
